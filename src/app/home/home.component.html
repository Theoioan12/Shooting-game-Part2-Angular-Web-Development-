Welcome to UFO Battle!

Get ready for an exciting UFO Battle! Take control of your spaceship, eliminate UFOs, and aim for the high score.

How to Play: Use the arrow keys to move your spaceship. Press the spacebar to shoot missiles and defend against the UFO invasion. Aim carefully to hit as many UFOs as possible.

Objective: Score points by shooting down UFOs. Be strategic and avoid getting hit. Your mission is to achieve the highest score and secure your place in the Records!
